Does this sound familiar?

Most founders are great at their business. Running the admin around it is a different story.

This is a systems problem and systems problems have solutions. Here is what happens when operations fall out of alignment and how I fix them:

01

Disconnected Systems & Spreadsheets

The Problem: You have data sitting in five different sheets. None of them talks to each other. Answering a simple question about your own business takes an hour of digging.

The Solution: Building custom financial and operational dashboards that centralise your data, making it easy to get the information you need.

02

No real picture of your finances

The Problem: You know roughly what came in. You are less sure what went out. The books are a few months behind, and tax season is not slowing down for anyone.

The Solution: Having ongoing reconciliations and accurate bookkeeping that give you a real picture of your numbers at all times.

03

Manual work eating your week

The Problem: Copying data between systems by hand. Chasing the same invoices every month. Sending the same emails over and over. Time that should be going into growing your business

The Solution:

The Solution: Streamlining workflows and creating automations that eliminate the busywork.

04

The business is running you

The Problem: You open your laptop and immediately feel behind. There is no system, just a pile of things demanding attention. You are reacting, not leading.

The Solution: Structuring administrative support that restores order and keeps your processes running smoothly.
