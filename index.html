About me

I'm Monicah — and I genuinely love this work.

I studied Economics and Mathematics at Kenyatta University because I was fascinated by how numbers tell stories. That hasn't changed — it's actually grown into something I now do professionally every day.

At Pitch Investors, I was deep in the numbers for 10+ businesses at a time — building financial models, running DCF valuations, and translating complex data into investor proposals that non-technical audiences could actually act on. Those businesses secured over $500k in investor interest. That result still motivates me.

At Aspire Lending, I saw that senior staff were losing hours every week to slow manual reports. I redesigned the process without being asked — and cut report prep time by 40%, freeing over 20 hours per week. That's how I work: I notice what's not working and I fix it.

I've trained with Athena — one of the most rigorous executive VA programs globally — which gave me real foundations in how to support busy executives: not just completing tasks, but anticipating needs and thinking three steps ahead.

I'm currently doing my MSc in Financial Engineering at WorldQuant University — which deepens exactly what I bring to clients: quantitative thinking, financial modeling, and data-driven decision making.

Outside of work, I care deeply about sustainable business and climate-conscious finance. I'm especially drawn to working with purpose-driven businesses.
